Target Audience:

The target audience will be made up of all residence of Southeastern Idaho. Adults are the main users of the site, however, teenagers, and older children will occastionally have need to use it. Most people check weather conditions on a regular basis when planning their various outdoor activities and types of trips.

Personas

Adult Male

Age Group

40 to 55 years-old

Main Needs and Expectations for the Site

The website would need to have accurate and up-to-date weather information. Easy to find tempertures, precipitation, hourly forcasts, and weekly forecasts.

How the Site Will Be Used

The site will be used quickly as a reference. It will probably not be used much as a stories of interest website.

Important Features and Functionality

The website needs to be quick and easy to find the main information. It would be helpful if all relavant informaiton was on the main page, with details easy to find with one click. It might also be good to send mobile alrets and weather warnings based on GPS cordinates.

Goals and Values of User

To be able to plan commutes, trips, hunting, fishing, and other outdoor acitivities quickly.

Scenarios

Scenario 1 - The Roadtrip Travelers

A mother of a family is planning a road trip this week. She is wondering the best days to travel on the road. It is winter, and she is hoping to avoid dangerous road conditions. She looks on the website for a 5-day forecast to determine which days to avoid.

Scenario 2 - The Commuter

A businessman heard that a storm was coming into town. He has an important meeting in the afternoon, but doesn't want to spend a lot of time in traffic. He is wondering if he could delay his communte or leave earier to avoid the downpour. The Climate Lowdown Website will give him hour by hour weather conditions.

Scenario 3 - The Event Planner

A wedding planner has a large and expensive outdoor wedding planned at the end of the week. It is springtime when there is much rain. If she is aware of inclimate weather far enough in advance, she can change the wedding to an indoor venue. She goes to the website to find the forecast for the weekend.

Scenario 4 - Parents of School Children

A father has an important busiess meeting tomorrow in the afternoon. Because the walk home from the school is a bit long, usually he picks up his kids from school when it is raining or snowing. If it is snowing during his meeting, he would like to make other pick-up arrangements for his kids. If the weather is nice, he will not need to worry about it since the kids can walk home. He checks the Climate Lowdown website to see the forecast for that particular time.

Scenario 5 - Farmers

A farmer has not completely picked his apple crop. The weather seems to be getting colder really fast. He goes to the Climate Lowdown webpage to see if there will be a hard freeze during the week. If there is, he will need to hurry and pick his remaing crop.
