Website Name:

The Climate Lowdown

Site Purpose:

The purpose of The Climate Lowdown Website is to provide accurate informaiton on weather conditions to a variety of people. The site will be a resource to south-eastern Idaho so that the audience will be able to safely plan their various outdoor activities and traveling events. In addition, the site will have interesting weather stories for the following cities: Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven.
