Localized Cold Front Freezes Lake in Time for Hockey Game

Localized Cold Front Freezes Lake in Time for Hockey Game

A radical cold front breifly decsended on Soda Springs Pond yesterday causing the entire body of water to freeze solid. Meteorolgists are baffled by the quick moving extremely small cold front that materialized and then disappaited over Soda Springs Pond. George Weatherman, a local broadcaster for Channel 11, was flabergasted saying, "I have never seen anything like this. It's as if the Boys Championship Hockey Game just had to happen!"

Many residents claim that this is proof that hockey is a divine sport. Alice Jumper, whose son is on the Soda Spring Boys Hockey Team, said, "This is proof that hockey games are a matter of great signficance. No one will ever say, 'It's just a game' anymore."

A cold front in extreme is potentially dangerous for surrounding areas. However, state farm inspectors say that no farms were damaged or touched. Jacob Plowman, a neighboring farmer, claimed the cold front didn't harm is crops at all. "And now I get to sit back and watch the championship game from my porch." The original game had been postponed indefinitely until the city arean's zambogy was repaired. The game has now been moved to Soda Springs Pond which has inexplicable remained frozen.
