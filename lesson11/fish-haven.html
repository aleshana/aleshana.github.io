Falling Fish from Sky Populate Local Reservoir

The fish hole of residents from Fish Haven was suddenly stocked with new fish from an unlikely source. People close to Fish Haven Reservoir reported a large dark cloud suddenly hovering over the water. Suddenly, the cloud began to rain fish. The cloud continued to shower the lake with what appeared to be rainbow trout, a favorite fish among residents.

The National Forest Service was there today counting and sampling the fish. Roger Forester, a wild life and fish expert from the forest service said, "these are the biggest and tastiet fish I have ever seen. Adding that there is just the right fish population for a healthy fish environment."

The State of Idaho announced earlier this year, that Fish Haven Reservoir would not be stocked with year due to budget cuts. Many residents and tourists who frequent the beloved fish whole had unsuccessfully petitioned the state to change their stance. Local resident, Janet Salmon, joyful proclaimed, "My family will be able to eat fish all year now."
