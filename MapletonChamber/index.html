Business Spotlight

Family Pet Hospital

Is your dog sick? Does your cat need his shots? Well then, the prefect place is the Mapleton's own pet hospital. The Family Pet Hospital located at 448 North on Highway 89 is a full-service clinic for animals. The staff is professional and friendly. You beloved companion is in good hands. With each clinician a pet lover, they understand how important good care and service is to each and every owner.

Over the years as Mapleton has grown, the hospital has stayed busy with animals from both ends of the spectrum. It is well equipped to meet all animal needs. They specialize in everything from dental care, diet consulting, and surgery. They have their own lab and facilities to house animals for longer-term care.

If you are still in doubt, just check out testimonials from their clients and patients. Even the animals agree that Family Pet Hospital is a great contribution to our community.
